Fapecargo (FAmous PEople CARd GO) is a geolocation-based card collecting game inspired by Pokemon Go.

Explore the real world to capture biographical cards of famous personalities linked to historical places: birthplaces, workplaces, graves...

<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
<li>Interactive OpenStreetMap with nearby cards</li>
<li>Original mini-games using device sensors:
  - Shake the apple tree (accelerometer)
  - Defuse the bomb (accelerometer)
  - Light the fire (microphone + camera)
</li>
<li>Personal collection with possession history</li>
<li>Card transfer between players</li>
<li>Player account with authentication</li>
</ul>

Cards are placed near locations linked to famous people from Wikidata. Get close to capture them!
